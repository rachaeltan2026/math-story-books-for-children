Primary levels and story-based math topics

Every book is crafted for young learners with clear goals, play-based examples, and story settings that make math meaningful.

Primary 1

Beginning number sense through counting games, shape hunts, and gentle story problems that build confidence.

  • Numbers & counting
  • Basic shapes
  • Patterns & comparisons

Primary 2

Introduction to addition, subtraction, measurement, and simple fractions inside relatable stories.

  • Simple addition/subtraction
  • Time & length
  • Half, quarter, and sharing

Primary 3

Strengthen problem solving with multiplication, division, fractions, geometry, and story-based challenges.

  • Multiplication & division
  • Fractions & measurement
  • Shape properties
